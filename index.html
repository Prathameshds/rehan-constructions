Management System

Powerful Features for
Modern Construction Firms

Deal Management

Track all your construction deals, project estimates, advances, and balances in one place with real-time updates.

Worker Management

Manage worker payments, track paid and remaining amounts, and assign workers to specific projects.

Material Tracking

Monitor material expenses, lease agreements for slab plates, column prox, mixer machines and more.

Financial Reports

Generate detailed profit/loss statements, export to PDF and Excel with advanced filtering options.

Advanced Filtering

Filter data by week, month, year, or custom date ranges. Filter by project for precise reporting.

Secure Authentication

User-based data isolation powered by Firebase. Each user's data is securely stored and accessible only to them.
